About Us

At Rushwave, we’re driven by a passion for extreme sports and the thrill of conquering the ocean. Founded by a team of adventure enthusiasts, we’re dedicated to bringing the world of kitesurfing to life through unforgettable experiences and adrenaline-fueled events. Our mission is to inspire riders of all levels to push their limits and embrace the rush of the waves. Join us as we celebrate the community that make up the heart of Rushwave.

At Rushwave, our team is made up of skilled instructors and seasoned athletes, dedicated to sharing their knowledge and passion for extreme sports. We believe that everyone can connect with nature and unlock their true potential in the ocean. Alongside events and competitions, we offer personalized training sessions that prioritize safety and progressive learning.

Our compromise with kitesurf

Our commitment at Rushwave is to provide an unforgettable, safe, and empowering experience for every participant. We prioritize sustainable practices, respecting the ocean and its surroundings in every event. Through expert guidance and a passion-driven approach, we aim to inspire confidence, resilience, and a deep connection to the sport. Join us in embracing the thrill of the waves while preserving the beauty of our natural playground.
